About Kicks for CHiPS

Kicks for CHiPS is an annual high school soccer tournament in Brooklyn dedicated to raising funds for CHiPS (Christian Help in Park Slope), a nonprofit serving the Gowanus community for over 50 years.

Founded by three Saint Ann's students, the tournament brings together high schoolers across Brooklyn to compete in a 5v5 double-elimination bracket.
