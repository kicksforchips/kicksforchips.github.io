The Tournament

About Kicks for CHiPS

May 31, 2026
J.J. Byrne Playground, Brooklyn

Kicks for CHiPS is an annual high school soccer tournament in Brooklyn dedicated to raising funds for CHiPS (Community Help in Park Slope), a nonprofit serving the Gowanus community for over 50 years.

Founded by Saint Ann's students Repin Minoff, Leif Moen, and Dempsey Gonya, the tournament brings together high schoolers across Brooklyn to compete in a 5v5 double-elimination bracket.
